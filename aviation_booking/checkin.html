TERMS & Conditions

Check online log unto aviation_booking/checkin.html and use the online check-in option. You’ll be required to provide your Booking Reference, first name, and surname and read the terms and conditions to proceed. Once this is done, passengers must print their boarding pass online and proceed to the airport. Online check-in is available on all Aviation Booking tickects from 24hours up to 3 hours before the scheduled departure time. Passengers are required to produce a valid national photo ID at the screening point and at the boarding gate. Acceptable IDs are Passport, National ID Card, Non-resident ID card, Driver’s License and National Health Insurance Card. Any passenger without identification will be refused travel. Passengers with checked bags must drop them off at the designated bag drop counter prior to counter closure. The Check-in counter closes 40min before departure and once the counter is closed, no bags can be accepted for travel. For more terms access about us page.
